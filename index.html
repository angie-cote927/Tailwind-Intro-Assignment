Princess Bubblegum

Ruler of the Candy Kingdom

Princess Bubblegum is the ruler of the Candy Kingdom, as well as a scientist who creates the people of her kingdom. She is very loved, and feared, by the people of her kingdom, but they choose time and time again to vote her into power!

Flame Princess

Ruler of the Flame Kingdom

Flame Princess is the new ruler of the Flame Kingdom. She overthrew her father, who had kept her locked up in a jar essentially using her as a chandelier previously. She has made the Flame Kingdom much better, and although she is very hot-headed a lot of the time (pun intended), she only does what is best for her people.

Raggedy Princess

Ruler of the Raggenbone Kingdom

Raggedy Princess is the ruler of the Ooo Junkyard, or as she calls it "Raggenbone Kingdom". Not a lot is known about her, although we do know that she owed Peanut Princess socks and some jorts in one of the episodes...
